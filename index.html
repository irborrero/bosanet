What is Bosanet?

Bosanet is a Business Intelligence Platform, bringing together Entrepreneurs and worldwide Investors.

Is my information secure?

Bosanet signs a confidentiality agreement that protects your business idea as well as all your information.

What is the scope of Bosanet's support?

Until your business idea is ready to be presented to Investors, Bosanet's Experts Team supports your company's development in the following areas: legal, financial, marketing, sales, technological, and human resources.

Do I have to pay if I want to explore the platform?

Bosanet is completly free in the information input stage. Once the information input is complete, the Experts Team evaluation stage starts and will show you the action plan to prepare your idea to be presented to Investors.

Who should I contact to start?

Bosanet is fully digital, with worldwide, easy web access. Just join the platform for free and start.

What should I do when an Investor is interested in my business idea?

Bosanet leads and supports you through the whole process until the end of the negotiation.

What do I have access to as an Investor?

Bosanet presents certified and fully designed business opportunities, as well as business ideas in development stage, to the Investors. Each opportunity has all the basic information required for investment analysis. After that point, Bosanet will be in touch with the Investor for the negotiation and closing stages.

Is Bosanet a crowdfunding?

Currently, Bosanet is a private investment platform. Later on, Bosanet will offer direct investment opportunities.
